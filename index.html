About AndyHQ

AndyHQ is the solo indie studio behind Overtime Live, a free real-time overtime tracking app for shift workers on iOS and Android. Overtime Live was built because over 85% of UK shift workers still track overtime manually — with spreadsheets or paper.

The studio also builds free web tools including the Sun Hours solar panel kWh calculator, designed for homeowners who want to understand their solar system's real-world output without paying for a professional estimate.

All tools are built with a simple principle: solve one problem, solve it well, and keep it free.
